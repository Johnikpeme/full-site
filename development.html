Coming Soon: Publishing

We partner with developers to refine, scale, and publish globally competitive games.

Ecosystemsupport.

Africa is the fastest-growing gaming market globally, with 8.9% YoY growth, over 200 million gamers, and a population of 1.5 billion—half under 19. Yet the continent captures just $7.7B of the $187B global games market, with only ~3% of revenue retained by African studios.

The challenge is structural: limited funding, few Africa-focused publishers, and weak global distribution. As a result, 64% of African-developed games earn under $5,000 in lifetime revenue, despite rising global demand for culturally relevant titles. This creates a clear opportunity to build world-class African games for a global audience.
