Our Story

About

Sharing African stories to the world, one game at a time!

At Dash Studios, we are building a gaming powerhouse to amplify African stories curated by talented African developers by optimizing and distributing their games to the hands of millions of African gamers and gamers around the world. We aim to achieve this in the following ways:

  • In-house game development.
  • Games publishing (Marketing, Distribution & Localisation).
  • Growth Partnerships

Dash Studios was established to address the clear gaps in the African gaming ecosystem. For years, Africans have primarily engaged with foreign games, leading to the growth of a vast community of millions of gamers. However, there is now a strong demand for culturally relevant, high-quality games tailored to African mobile gamers. We believe that the talent within Africa is ready to be nurtured and supported to bridge this gap.

Game Publishing (Coming Soon)

We take a partnership approach to game publishing, collaborating with talented African game developers/studios to offer the much-needed support to optimize their title and become market ready. Our support includes:

  • Conceptualization and incubation.
  • Production and games economics.
  • Marketing and distribution.
  • Localization and many more!.

If you are interested in partnering with us to take your game to the next level, please reach out to partnerships@dashstudios.tech.
