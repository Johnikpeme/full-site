A Nation Divided

Nation Divided is a compelling narrative-driven game that immerses players in the complex and tumultuous story of the Nigerian Civil War, also known as the Biafran War. The game offers a multifaceted perspective, allowing players to experience the conflict through the eyes of various characters from different backgrounds and regions. Players navigate the intricate political, social, and personal challenges of the era, uncovering the rich tapestry of stories and struggles that shaped this pivotal moment in history. With its focus on historical accuracy and emotional depth, Nation Divided provides an engaging and educational exploration of one of Nigeria's most significant historical events. Currently in development for PC & Console.
